E-Waste-

E-waste is electronic product that are unwanted and not working properly,Which have hazardous substances that can pose severe risks to humanm and environmental health .

Problem:

Genrally we have a collection of old and useless electronic items that we throw them in dustbins or sell to scrap dealers. bot have you have wondered where all the stuff ends up? Well that depends on how you can rid it. In 2014 nearly 60% of old electronics were dumped into landfills the other 40% was recycled and 25% of that,was then shipped to a developing nation. Electronic waste or E-waste is now the fastest growing source of toxic waste in the world. In 2016,44.7 million tons of electronic waste was generated worldwide or the equivalent of 4,500 Eiffel Towers.

solution:

E-waste is not really junk . last year the UN estimated e-waste could contain more than 67 billion worth of precious metals. and while wealthy countries generate much of this waste . The developing nations are often left with the task of recycling it .
